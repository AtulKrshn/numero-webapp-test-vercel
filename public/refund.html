We guarantee that you will receive your personalized PDF report within 12 hours of successful payment.

2. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Due to the nature of digital products (personalized PDF files), we generally cannot offer refunds once the report has been successfully generated and delivered to your email address.

  • Result dissatisfaction (Numerology is an interpretive art/science).

  • Change of mind after the report has been delivered.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for non-delivery:

  1. Email us at support@sankhya.com with your Order ID or the email address used for purchase.
  2. State "Refund Request" in the subject line.

Our support team will verify if the email was delivered. If we confirm non-delivery or a system error on our end, we will process your refund immediately.

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed back to your original method of payment (via Razorpay) within 5-7 business days.
