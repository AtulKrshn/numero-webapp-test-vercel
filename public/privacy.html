Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At DevSankhya Vedic Numerology, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to generate your astrological and numerological report:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Time of Birth (Optional)
  • Place of Birth
  • Email Address (for delivery)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your details are used strictly for:

  • Calculating your Vedic, Lo Shu, and Chaldean charts.
  • Generating your personalized PDF report.
  • Delivering the report to your email address.

3. Data Retention & Deletion ("Calculate & Delete")

We adhere to a strict Data Deletion Policy. Once your report has been successfully generated and delivered, your birth details are retained for a maximum of 24 hours (in case of delivery failure/retry) and then permanently deleted from our processing servers.

We do not store your personal charts historical data for future use.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We do not share your birth details with any third-party marketing agencies.

5. Payment Information

We do not store your credit card or payment information. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway provider (Razorpay), which adheres to international security standards (PCI-DSS).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@devsankhya.com.
